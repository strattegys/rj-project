Capabilities

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A focused toolkit for teams who value uptime, security, and operational intelligence.

Continuous Heartbeat

Polls Prometheus, Kubernetes, Elasticsearch, Grafana, cloud APIs, and custom endpoints on a configurable cadence. Tracks state between polls to detect deltas and trends.

Automated Investigation

When an anomaly is detected, the agent pulls logs, checks recent deploys, walks the dependency graph, queries past incidents, and builds a root cause hypothesis backed by evidence.

Feedback Learning Loop

Learns from explicit feedback (was this useful?) and implicit signals (what did you do next?). Gets more accurate for your specific environment over time. Never makes the same mistake twice.

Proactive Trend Detection

Spots slow-moving problems humans miss: disk usage growing 2%/week, latency creeping up, intermittent failures accelerating. Warns you before thresholds fire.

Intelligent Escalation

Routes to the right team at the right severity with full context: summary, evidence chain, confidence score, and suggested remediation. Severity and routing calibrate from feedback.

Weekly Intelligence Reports

Automated "State of Infrastructure" digests: emerging trends, correlation discoveries, prediction warnings, accuracy metrics, and noise reduction stats.

How It Works

From deployment to value in minutes

1

Install the agent

Add VigilIT to your AI tool of choice — Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or any agent harness. Tell it what to watch. It connects to Prometheus, Grafana, Elasticsearch, Kubernetes, and cloud providers automatically.

2

Baseline & learn

The heartbeat begins. VigilIT polls your infrastructure, builds a model of what "normal" looks like for each service — traffic patterns, error rates, latency profiles, resource usage.

3

Detect & investigate

When the agent spots an anomaly, it automatically triages severity, checks recent changes, correlates signals, and builds a root cause hypothesis — all with cited evidence.

4

Escalate with context

The right team gets notified via Slack, PagerDuty, or Jira with a complete incident packet: what happened, why, what to do, and how confident the agent is in its diagnosis.

5

Feedback & improve

After every incident, VigilIT learns from your team's feedback and actions. It gets smarter every week — better triage, fewer false positives, deeper institutional knowledge.
