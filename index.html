How VigilIT Works

1

Connect Your Stack

Point VigilIT at your existing monitoring tools — Prometheus, Grafana, Elasticsearch, Kubernetes, cloud providers. No agents to install, no data migration. Works with what you already have.

2

The Heartbeat Starts

VigilIT begins polling your infrastructure on a regular cadence. It builds a baseline of what "normal" looks like — traffic patterns, error rates, latency profiles, resource usage.

3

Detect & Investigate

When the agent spots an anomaly, it automatically triages and investigates — checking logs, recent changes, dependencies, and past incidents to build a root cause hypothesis backed by evidence.

4

Escalate With Context

The right team gets notified with a full incident summary, evidence chain, and suggested fix. No more "CPU is high" alerts with zero context.

5

Learn & Improve

After every incident, VigilIT learns from your feedback and your team's actions. False positive? It won't make that mistake again. Good catch? It'll look for similar patterns proactively.

🛡

Your Data. Your Network. Period.

VigilIT runs entirely on your infrastructure using local AI models. No cloud LLM APIs, no telemetry phone-home, no data exfiltration risk. Built for organizations where security isn't optional.
